About NHCA

Who We Are

The NHCA Pangasinan Chapter is a vibrant community of Honda Click motorcycle enthusiasts based in Pangasinan, Philippines. We are dedicated to promoting safe riding, sharing passion for motorcycles, and building lasting friendships within our region.

Our members are riders from all walks of life in Pangasinan, united by their love for Honda Click bikes and the open road. We organize regular meetups, group rides, and community events throughout the province. Above all, we are passionate about giving back to our community through charity rides and helping those in need, because we believe that being a part of a motorcycle club means being a responsible and caring member of society.
